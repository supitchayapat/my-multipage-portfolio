Where Did I Study?

I spent my time in college at two Universities in the Midwest.

University of Notre Dame

Master of Science in Technology Entrepreneurship (ESTEEM)

While at the University of Notre Dame, I earned a degree in Engineering, Science and Technology Entrepreneurship. The graduate program taught me how to implement the technical knowledge I learned while I was an undergraduate through working with a startup company for my thesis. My thesis included scientific research, healthcare market analysis, a business plan, launch strategy and financial forecasts for Somni's sleep improvement program.

Pitched in Startup Weekend Notre Dame

Graduated May 2016
Michigan State University

Bachelor of Science in Applied Engineering Sciences (AES)

While at Michigan State University, I earned a degree in Applied Engineering Sciences, which is a combination of business and engineering. My degree has two concentrations; Computer Science through the engineering department and Supply Chain Management through the Eli Broad College of Business, which has the #1 Supply Chain Management program in the United States.

Winner of "Most Sustainable" Capstone Thesis Award

Graduated May 2015
