Where Have I Worked?

You name it... I've probably got some experience in it.

Models Plus, LLC

Product Engineer

While I worked at Models Plus, I successfully led engineering design, drafting and 3D printing for 34 projects in 9 months. I designed a handheld orthodontic product for injection molded manufacturing using SolidWorks and 3D printing. I modeled and completed engineering drawings for an AR15 bolt carrier for production level CNC machining of up to 1,800 units per month. I designed and tested fixtures for hard coating and metalizing both sides of 3 automotive emblems.

In addition to my responsibilities as an engineer, I was also involved in business improvement. I built product databases for analyzing labor and material costs, which resulted in the implementation of new molding techniques that reduced labor and materials by 60% and 20%, respectively. I mapped two buildings for relocating machinery to improve employee foot traffic. I orchestrated the development of an employee appreciation program for recognizing great employees.

Skills Learned: SolidWorks, Fixtures, Casting, Molding, Vacuum Forming, Laser Cutting
